Stock Market Basics

What is Fundamental Analysis?

Fundamental analysis is a method of evaluating a stock's intrinsic value by analyzing various economic and financial factors. This includes:

  • Company's financial statements
  • Economic indicators
  • Industry conditions
  • Overall market conditions

Key Financial Ratios

Price-to-Earnings (P/E) Ratio

Formula: Stock Price / Earnings per Share

Indicates how much investors are willing to pay for â‚¹1 of company earnings

Return on Equity (ROE)

Formula: Net Income / Shareholders' Equity

Measures a company's profitability in relation to shareholders' equity

Debt-to-Equity Ratio

Formula: Total Liabilities / Shareholders' Equity

Shows the proportion of equity and debt used to finance assets
